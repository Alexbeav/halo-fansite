The worlds and installations of the Halo universe

Planet

Reach

The UNSC's fortress world and primary military hub. Home to the Spartan-II program and the site of one of humanity's greatest battles. Its fall marked a turning point in the Human-Covenant War.

Epsilon Eridani System Population: 703 Million (former)
Planet

Earth

Humanity's homeworld and the heart of the United Nations Space Command. The Covenant's invasion of Earth was their boldest assault yet.

Sol System UNSC Headquarters
Starship

UNSC Pillar of Autumn

A Halcyon-class light cruiser commanded by Captain Jacob Keyes. This ship carried Master Chief and Cortana to the first Halo ring.

Halcyon-class Cruiser Captain: Jacob Keyes
Forerunner

Installation 04

The first Halo ring discovered by humanity. A massive Forerunner superweapon designed to eliminate the Flood threat by destroying all sentient life in the galaxy.

Halo Ring Diameter: 10,000 km
